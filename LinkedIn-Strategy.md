# LinkedIn Growth Strategy

## Objective

Build awareness of RAY as:

* An AI company
* A top employer
* A thought leader in AI operations

## Content Pillars

### 1. Founder Insights

Thought leadership posts from company leaders.

### 2. AI Trends

Commentary on developments in AI and automation.

### 3. Employee Stories

Highlight team members and their growth.

### 4. Company Culture

Show what it's like to work at RAY.

### 5. Wins & Milestones

Celebrate achievements and business progress.
