## Founder LinkedIn Post Example

### The Biggest Mistake Companies Make With AI

Most companies don't have an AI problem.

They have a process problem.

AI doesn't magically fix broken workflows, poor documentation, or unclear ownership.

The companies seeing the biggest gains from AI aren't necessarily using the most advanced models. They're the ones that know exactly where time is being lost and have designed systems around solving those bottlenecks.

At RAY, we see this every day.

The future belongs to businesses that combine great people, great processes, and great AI tools.

What bottleneck in your business would you eliminate first?
